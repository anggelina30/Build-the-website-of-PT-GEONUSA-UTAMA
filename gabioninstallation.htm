Slope Stability

Gabion is a welded wire cage or box filled with materials such as stone, concrete, sand, or soil that is installed on the slope like a block construction. The purpose of gabion installation is to help improving slope stability, preventing land slide, and giving erosion protection in slope.

Gabion (Terramesh)

Generally, we use regular gabion or Terramesh System:

  • Regular Gabion
    • Gabion are baskets made of double twisted steel woven wire mesh. Gabions are filled with stones at the project site to form flexible, permeable, monolithic structures such as retaining walls, channel linings, and weirs for erosion control projects. The steel wire used in the manufacture of the gabion is Galmac coated (Zn-5%Al). A PVC coating is then applied to provide added protection for use in aggressive environments (salt water, fresh water, presence of acids) or wherever the risk of corrosion is present.

    Gabion

  • Terramesh® System (TMS)
    • Terramesh® System (TMS) is a modular system where the process is to make a vertical construction used for soil reinforcement made from previously assembled units made with double wire hexagonal wiremesh. The reinforcement base panel, front panel and gabion box lid of the Terramesh System are manufactured from one continuous panel of mesh; the back panel is factory connected to the base panel. The facing section of the unit is formed by connecting the back panel and diaphragms to the main unit; the gabion facing is divided into uniformly partitioned cells by internal diaphragms.

    Terramesh

All of the gabion wiremesh materials are supplied from PT. Maccaferri Indonesia.

Additional material:

  • Geogrid
  • Stone fills
