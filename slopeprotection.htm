Slope Protection (Rockfall Protection) with Wiremesh

Rockfall protection with using Steelgrid wiremesh is a solution of applying slope protection from falling rocks with active method (with anchors) and passive methods (without anchors on slopes). Protection will be applied on the slope surface so that loose material on the slope surface does not fall and endanger the road users. If necessary, a combination of wiremesh installation and soil nailing systems can be applied if the slope needs additional reinforcement.

Slope Protection with Erosion Control

Erosion control is a method of providing slope protection from erosion so the quality of the soil can be retained in order to prevent land slide and decay. Erosion control is implemented by providing media for plants to grow until the roots grow to the slope surface. This will help maintaining the quality of the soil naturally. If necessary, a combination of vegetation and soil nailing systems can be applied if the slope needs additional reinforcement.

MacMat

Macmat® is the latest generation of high performing geomat made from UV stabilized HDPE. The polymeric geomat controls surface erosion and helps to propagate the vegetation. Main application areas are cut slope protection, erosion control & stabilization.
The solution aims to provide a measure which will mitigate the effects of the weathering agents in the short terms in order to enhance the growth of the vegetation onto the exposed slopes. Upon heavy rains and water flows, the reinforcing ability of the mat prevents erosion or the otherwise vulnerable vegetation. Once, vegetated, erosion control mat reduces runoff velocities and thereby increases infiltration and reduces discharge. Steel pins are used to secure the Macmat® EM to the slope surface.
We can use either MacMat EM (without steel wire) or MacMat HS (with steel wire) depends on whether the slope needs additional reinforcement or not.
