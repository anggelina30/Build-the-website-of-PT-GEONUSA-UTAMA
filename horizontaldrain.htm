Slope Stability

Horizontal Drainage

Horizontal drain is generally established for accommodating underground seepage that relief out water from the slope bank failure zones. This will help increasing the stability of slope.



Common Scope of Works

  • Horizontal Drilling work for drain holes use hydraulics rotary drilling equipment that is suitable to the soil condition at project site and will be able to meet high productivity standard in achieving the work target.
  • Installation of drain pipes and accessories into drain holes.
  • Providing skilled workers for drilling works that fill all Safety requirements for this particular works and as a standard practice in the project sites.
  • Providing accommodations for the workers and transportation facilities including in Mobilization and Demobilization.
  • All other preparation works before commencing the work if needed

Technical Specifications

  • The drain holes diameter is 89mm - 150mm.
  • The Length of drain holes can be achieved up to 150 meter length.
  • Drilling inclination is 10 degrees upward direction.
  • The drain pipe is PVC 1.5" pipe size.
  • The PVC pipe shall be perforated with 8mm holes or slotted in three rows with 100mm center spacing except at area from failure lines to finished fill slope surface the outlet end is not perforated.
  • The perforated section of pipe shall be wrapped in geotextile non-woven material to provide a filter to exclude soil particle from the pipe.
  • The 500mm outlet end the pipes shall be grouted.

Drilling Method

Drilling equipment shall be provided and the type of equipment will be based on soil type at the project site. The drilling work is performed by water flush system. Before drilling can be started, it is important to position the rig properly. It must stand on firm bearing and lock the position by anchor the rig into the ground by steel bars. Sand/soil bags may be used to align the bench surface. To ensure proper alignment, mast shall be checked from two directions before and throughout drilling work.

As the drilling progresses, a suitable length of casing shall be installed into the hole to prevent soil collapsing. The rig position is located on bench level. Drilling mast direction is adjustable as required angle of drilling path. Any additional platform or support might be required if the actual bench width is less than our rig length.

Drilling can be started using drilling bit 2.5" (OD 73mm) diameter size with guide pipe to maintain hole alignment. Drilling bit is driven by 1.5" drilling rod which transfers torque and force from drilling machine. Suitable length of 2.5"-3" casing will be installed to protect hole collapses. The required length of casing might vary depend on the soil stability encountered. The sufficient casing length is provided onsite to allow full casing drilling operation. Drilling rig will always be monitored during drilling works by checking the mast direction. The hole protection casing is installed according to drilling progress. Drilling can be stopped after reach designed depth.



Cleaning

Upon completion of the drilling, the bottom of the trench will be cleaned by water flush. The process continues until the sediment mud or sand clean out from the hole.

Drain Pipes Installation

Pipe perforation, geotextile wrap and bottom cap is prefabricated on site. Drain pipe is inserted into the hole after bottom hole is cleaned. Pipe installation must be done slowly and carefully to prevent excessive damage to the hole wall. When the pipe installed at required length, a cement bag and/or cloth is inserted into the hole to plug the gap between the pipe and soil in order to prevent the following cement grout plug to enter the perforated zone. The cement grout is installed at last 500mm drill section from ground surface
